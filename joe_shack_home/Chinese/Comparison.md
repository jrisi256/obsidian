In Chinese, you can compare things using bǐ.

Examples:
* **Nǐ bǐ wǒ hǎo** --> You are better than me.
* **Nǐ bǐ wǒ gāo** --> You are taller than me.
* **Wǒ juédé déguó cài bǐ yīngguó cài hǎo**. --> I think German food is better than British food.
